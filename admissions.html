Join Leads Community School

We welcome students from all backgrounds to apply to Leads Community School. Our admission process is transparent, fair, and designed to identify students with a passion for learning and growth.

Explore our requirements, submission process, and key dates to secure your child's place in our vibrant academic community.

Admission Requirements

Applicants must provide the following documents:

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or proof of age
  • Previous school reports (if applicable)
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Medical clearance certificate

Admission Process

The admission process includes the following steps:

  1. Submit completed application form and documents.
  2. Entrance assessment (for certain grades).
  3. Interview with student and parent/guardian.
  4. Admission decision and notification.
  5. Fee payment and enrollment confirmation.
