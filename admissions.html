Admission Requirements

To ensure a smooth and fair admission process, parents are required to provide:

  • Completed admission application form
  • Birth certificate or proof of age
  • Previous school report(s), where applicable
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
  • Medical clearance or health record

Admission Process

  1. Submission of completed application and required documents
  2. Entrance assessment (where applicable)
  3. Interaction with the student and parent/guardian
  4. Admission decision and notification
  5. Fee payment and enrollment confirmation

Once admitted, your child becomes part of a supportive learning community committed to growth, discipline, and excellence.
