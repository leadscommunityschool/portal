Our School Community

At Leads Community School, we value a strong, vibrant community that supports students, parents, and staff. We regularly organize events, workshops, and social activities to foster collaboration and growth.

Through partnerships, student clubs, and volunteer programs, our students experience a holistic education that extends beyond the classroom.

Community Activities

We organize a variety of community-focused activities, including:

  • Annual cultural and sports festivals
  • Student clubs and societies
  • Parent-teacher workshops and meetings
  • Environmental and social volunteer programs
  • Alumni networking events
