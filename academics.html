Our Academic Programs

At Leads Community School, we offer a wide range of academic programs that challenge and inspire students. Our curriculum aligns with the Ghana Education Service standards while emphasizing creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving.

We believe that a strong academic foundation, coupled with extracurricular development, prepares students to excel in higher education and beyond.
